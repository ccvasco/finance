Which metric is "best"?



Each provides different insight:

Operating income shows whether the company's main business is profitable, without the effects of financing or taxes.
Net income shows the overall accounting profit attributable to shareholders.
Operating cash flow shows whether the business is actually bringing in cash from its operations.
Free cash flow shows how much cash remains after maintaining or expanding the business through capital expenditures.
